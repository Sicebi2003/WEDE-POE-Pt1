About Efficient Energy

Your trusted partner in petroleum solutions

Efficient Energy is a fully Black-owned business located just outside Johannesburg CBD, offering products throughout the South African region of SADC. Our founders have combined their skills within the business sector with favorable market conditions to meet the growing demand for reliable fuel supply.

Our vision is to expand beyond only providing wholesalers licenses and become the owner of the largest mining, storage facilities and distributors in the SADC region and Africa. We aim to impact the economy of South Africa through job opportunities and people empowerment.

Customer excellence sets EE apart from its competition. We are strategically linked to producers/suppliers such as Total SA, Sasol, Afrox, and FuelX, ensuring no interruption in the supply of our products.
